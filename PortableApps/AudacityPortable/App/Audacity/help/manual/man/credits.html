Brief History

Audacity was started in 1999 by Dominic Mazzoni while he was a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA (USA). He was working on a research project with his advisor, Professor Roger Dannenberg, and they needed a tool that would let them visualize audio analysis algorithms. Over time, this application developed into a general audio editor, and other people started helping out.

Today, Audacity is developed using the Git version control system hosted at GitHub. Dozens of people have contributed to Audacity, and progress is continually accelerating.
