Scrub Toolbar

From Audacity Development Manual
Jump to: navigation, search
Scrub Toolbar starts, stops or switches to Scrub play or Seek play, and hides or displays the Scrub Ruler.

Scrub Toolbar has the following buttons:

  • Scrub play button Clicking this button will start, stop or switch to Scrub play
  • Seek play button Clicking this button will start, stop or switch to Seek play
  • Scrub Ruler button Clicking this button displays or hides the Scrub Ruler
